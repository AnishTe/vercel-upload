Algo Limit Transfer

* Tip - The requested changes will take effect from the next trading day

Instructions

Please review the cases below to understand how the limit transfer works:

Case 1: Available Limit Equals Current Algo Limit

Current Algo Limit: ₹50,000

Available Limit: ₹50,000

Since the available limit matches the current Algo limit:

  • ₹50,000 will be transferred to Algo.
  • ₹0 will be allocated to other trading platforms.

Case 2: Available Limit Less Than Current Algo Limit

Current Algo Limit: ₹50,000

Available Limit: ₹30,000

In this case, the available limit is less than the current Algo limit:

  • ₹30,000 will be transferred to Algo (equal to the available limit).
  • ₹0 will be allocated to other trading platforms because there is no remaining limit.

Case 3: Available Limit Greater Than Current Algo Limit

Current Algo Limit: ₹50,000

Available Limit: ₹70,000

Here, the available limit exceeds the current Algo limit:

  • ₹50,000 will be transferred to Algo (up to the current Algo limit).
  • ₹20,000 (remaining limit) will be available for other trading platforms.
